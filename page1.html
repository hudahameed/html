Family






Parents

I live with both of my parents. My mother’s name is Iffat Hameed and my father’s name is Abdul Hameed. I have nice and stable relationships with both my parents. I’m really thankful to be their daughter. They care for me a lot and always give me the best life possible. My dad is a diplomat which means that he works for the Pakistani government. He’s funny, smart and head-strong. He likes reading and poetry. I get a bit of my sense of humour from him. My mom is a stay at home mom for now. She used to be a teacher back in Pakistan. She is the most caring person you’ll ever meet. She’s hilarious and optimistic. I like to go to her to vent and talk about my problems and then afterwards go to my dad for comedic relief sometimes. I got most of my sense of humour from him.








Siblings

I am the middle child of three kids. It explains a lot about why I am the way that I am. The middle child is always said to be the one who gets no attention. However, that is not true, most of the time. For me, I will either be getting the most attention from my parents or the least. Depending on what type of day it is or what is going on. I have an older sister, Sarah. She studies at the University of Toronto. I have a younger brother, Salman. He is in third grade. They are the ones who keep me calm and down to earth. We move around a lot and so can’t have friends who are physically capable of being there for me, so it’s important to get along with them. I don’t get along with Salman as much I do with Sarah. This is because he is more annoying than Sarah. Both of us are in the process of growing up. I didn’t even get along with Sarah up until her last year of high school.
